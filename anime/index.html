3 Man page

  • Items marked as DONE have been verified as good by at least 2 people. Otherwise they are TODO.
  • To mark it as done in EMACS, move cursor to item. Toggle C-c C-t until desired state is reached.
  • To mark your personal item as complete, move cursor on your name. Toggle C-c C-c until desired state is reached.
  • To edit the table, go to any row. Type in required information. Press TAB and you'll move to the next item; table will resize.
  • To create another column, move your cursor to any column, press M-S-left and a column will appear left of the cursor.
  • To move a column, move cursor to it and press M-right or M-left depending on where you want it to move.
